# Frontier Rejections

## Purpose

This file documents research directions that were **explicitly considered and bracketed off** from the current SUF package. They remain intellectually interesting but are excluded for stated reasons.

## Rejected directions

### 1. Quantum-consciousness connections

**Status:** Rejected for current package

**Reason:** Insufficient empirical anchor. While quantum biology and quantum cognition are active research areas, there is no established bridge between quantum phenomena and the macro-scale coordination systems SUF studies (institutional, social). Including this would add speculation without strengthening the core argument.

**Revisit condition:** If empirical evidence emerges linking quantum effects to institutional coordination or if the framework expands to neural substrate questions.

### 2. Computational/simulation hypotheses

**Status:** Rejected for current package

**Reason:** Premature at current proof stage. Simulation arguments (Bostrom-style) are metaphysically interesting but do not improve the operationalization of I/C/L/U in observable coordination systems. Including this risks philosophical distraction from empirical work.

**Revisit condition:** If the framework achieves strong predictive closure and seeks ontological foundations, or if simulation hypotheses become empirically testable.

### 3. AGI/final-theory inflation

**Status:** Rejected for current package

**Reason:** Risk of inflation and category error. SUF currently studies observable coordination systems (states, societies). AGI is not yet an observable empirical domain. Positioning SUF as a "framework for understanding AGI" would be speculation based on analogy rather than evidence.

**Revisit condition:** If AGI systems become empirically available as study objects, or if SUF achieves demonstrated cross-domain validity that genuinely includes artificial systems.

### 4. Universe-level limit cases

**Status:** Rejected for current package

**Reason:** Beyond observable scope. Applying "unity-like organization" to universe-level questions (cosmic coherence, universal integration) moves from bounded research to cosmological speculation. The universe is not a studyable coordination system in the same sense as a pandemic response apparatus.

**Revisit condition:** Not planned. This is considered a permanent boundary for SUF.

## Honesty function

This rejection list serves three purposes:

1. **Transparency:** Readers know what was excluded, not just what was included
2. **Scope discipline:** Prevents gradual creep into speculation under the guise of "extension"
3. **Future clarity:** If these topics reappear, they must meet explicit revisit conditions

## Relationship to framework

These rejections are consistent with SUF's bounded-release posture. The framework makes modest claims precisely because it excludes domains where it cannot yet claim empirical traction.
