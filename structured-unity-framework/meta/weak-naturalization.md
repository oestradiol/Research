# Weak Naturalization

## 1. Definition

**Weak naturalization** means: phenomenology **constrains** models and interpretation; metrics track **correspondence** and stability, not **identity** with lived experience; science extends the field of experience through disciplined methods rather than escaping it.

## 2. Why it matters in this framework

It blocks both **reductionism** (experience = data) and **insulation** (experience immune to inquiry). It keeps **Unity Dynamics** and the **Research Program** from pretending metrics are phenomenology.

## 3. What it permits

Operationalization; proxy families; comparators and nulls; cross-scale reading; revision of models when evidence shifts.

## 4. What it does not permit

Silent identification of **U** with **consciousness**; treating convenience metrics as **essence**; using science as a **substitute** for phenomenological discipline at the ground layer.

## 5. Relation to science and metrics

Science is **strong** where intersubjective validation holds; that strength does not erase the **epistemic starting point** of awareness for this framework’s structure.

## 6. Common misreadings

- “Weak naturalization = anti-science” — **No**: it re-situates science without naive scientism.  
- “Weak naturalization = no rigor” — **No**: rigor includes **claim typing** and **honest limits**.
