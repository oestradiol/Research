# emerges-from

Use this relation when a higher-order domain or phenomenon develops out of organized lower-level processes or earlier conceptual groundwork.

Example: [philosophy of science and model discipline](../research-map/00-foundations-and-methods/philosophy-of-science-and-model-discipline.md) `emerges-from` [phenomenology and epistemic grounding](../research-map/00-foundations-and-methods/phenomenology-and-epistemic-grounding.md).
