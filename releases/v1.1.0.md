## Summary

`v1.1.0` is the New Zealand monograph-baseline release of the full `Research/` repository. It publishes:

- [Structured Unity Framework](https://github.com/oestradiol/Research/tree/main/structured-unity-framework) as the academic core at the chapter-ready New Zealand baseline
- [Knowledge](https://github.com/oestradiol/Research/tree/main/knowledge) as the stable sibling atlas founded on SUF
- [Research Tools](https://github.com/oestradiol/Research/tree/main/tools) as the read-only validation and reporting layer

## Release highlights

- SUF now carries a chapter-ready New Zealand monograph baseline inside the locked windows while keeping the public route bounded and non-universal.
- The New Zealand route-control layer now includes direct DPMC action-plan, operating-system, public-information, and Level 4 review anchors.
- SUF now includes explicit New Zealand monograph chapter notes plus a monograph-facing table-and-figure plan.
- Taiwan remains the bounded `15`-event comparator; later cross-case closure phases remain deferred rather than silently implied.
