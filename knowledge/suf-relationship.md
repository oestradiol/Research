# SUF Relationship

## Purpose

This note explains how **Knowledge** relates to the sibling **Structured Unity Framework (SUF)** project.

Start here when you want to understand why the atlas is founded on SUF without assuming that SUF exhausts the package.

## What SUF contributes to Knowledge

SUF contributes most clearly as:

- a layered scaffold for moving across foundational, formal, applied, and reflexive zones
- a relation-sensitive way of connecting notes without collapsing them into one theory
- a route-building discipline for moving from scaffold nodes to applied coordination questions

In practice, that means SUF most strongly shapes:

- cluster ordering
- some typed relation choices
- the route layer
- a subset of hub nodes where organization, timing, coordination, or bridge logic matter especially strongly

## Where SUF is most useful here

SUF is especially helpful in Knowledge when:

- a topic depends on explicit relation structure rather than isolated definitions
- a route moves across several clusters and needs a coherent travel logic
- organization, scale, or coordination are more important than domain-specific micro-detail
- a note benefits from being placed inside a broader cross-domain map

## Where Knowledge goes beyond SUF

Knowledge is deliberately broader than SUF.

Many notes should eventually lean harder on:

- domain-native methods
- rival systems traditions
- disciplinary histories
- literatures that are only lightly related to SUF

That is a feature, not a failure. Knowledge is strongest when it lets SUF help where it is genuinely clarifying and then lets better local tools take over where they should.
