## Route rule

Routes are optional guides through the package. They should help navigation and comparison, not replace the notes themselves. Use them to move through the connected graph without flattening it into one path.

Route dependencies should be explicit in both properties and body links so they remain visible in Graph view and easy to inspect in Backlinks and Outgoing Links.
