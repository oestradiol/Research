# Contributor Guide

## 1. Purpose

This guide is for edits to the public **Knowledge** atlas.

## 2. Before editing

Read:

- `README.md`
- `Knowledge Package Spec.md`
- `_indexes/knowledge-index.md`
- `suf-relationship.md`

If your edit affects how Knowledge relates to SUF, also inspect the sibling SUF package surfaces linked from `suf-relationship.md`.

## 3. What is locked vs flexible

High-friction to change without discussion:

- the public identity of Knowledge as a sibling project founded on SUF
- the 9-cluster scaffold
- typed relation discipline
- the distinction between node graph and study routes

Flexible with care:

- wording clarity
- route additions
- deepening node anchors
- new cross-links
- metadata refinement that improves Obsidian use without bloating the vault

## 4. Editing rules

- keep Markdown body links as the primary graph backbone
- use properties for filtering and route metadata, not as a substitute for real links
- keep filenames safe and stable
- prefer typed relation links when relation meaning matters
- keep SUF advocacy honest and bounded

## 5. Route rules

- make route dependencies explicit in both properties and body links
- make route-to-node links easy to follow in Graph view
- avoid turning every route into a prerequisite for every other route

## 6. Node rules

- deepened hub nodes may grow richer anchor sets and route links
- most nodes should stay compact unless there is a clear reason to deepen them
- do not let the atlas turn into an unmanaged literature dump
