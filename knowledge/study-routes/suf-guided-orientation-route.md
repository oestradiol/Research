# SUF-Guided Orientation Route

## Purpose

This is the entry route for the Knowledge atlas. It introduces the graph through the layered scaffold that SUF makes easiest to see, while keeping space for other frameworks once you enter each cluster.

## Companion frameworks or lenses

Use domain-native frameworks freely inside each node. This route is intentionally permissive about leaving SUF in the background once it has done the main scaffold work.

## SUF hook / route note

SUF is strongest here as a connective map. This route is the clearest demonstration of why the two sibling projects belong together: SUF supplies the travel logic, while Knowledge supplies the broader terrain and competing literatures that keep that logic honest.
