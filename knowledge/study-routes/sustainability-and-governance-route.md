## Purpose

This route joins governance, intervention, and long-horizon systems thinking in order to study sustainability as a coordination and design problem rather than a slogan.

## What this route is trying to surface

- how long-horizon governance problems depend on system dynamics
- why intervention design and sustainability questions must be linked
- how ecological and social coordination pressures interact
- where SUF can help organize cross-scale governance thinking without replacing field-specific work

## Companion frameworks or lenses

Governance, sustainability, ecology, public policy, and systems literatures should all remain active on this route.

## SUF hook / route note

This is a strong applied route for SUF because sustainability problems often turn on coordination across scales, time windows, and partially coupled systems. That said, the route should stay accountable to domain-native sustainability and governance work rather than trying to subsume it.

## Expected outputs

- a stronger map from formal dynamics into applied governance
- a clearer sustainability path through the atlas
- better grounds for comparing SUF with neighboring intervention frameworks
