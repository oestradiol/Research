- how claims become evidentially disciplined
- how measurement choices shape later interpretation
- where proxy bundles help and where they distort
- why formal and applied readouts should stay tied to explicit assumptions

## SUF hook / route note

SUF is especially useful on this route when it keeps organizational claims tied to observability, proxy discipline, and explicit readout limits. This is one of the main places where SUF can stay both ambitious and honest.

- better route discipline around indicators and proxies
- stronger preparation for applied and comparative routes
