# Management and Organization Studies

## Cluster link

- [emerges-from](../../_relations/emerges-from.md) [07 Applied Design and Intervention](README.md)

## Study purpose

Study organizations, management, operational coordination, decision structures, and how institutions actually function under practical constraints.

## Key questions

- How do organizations coordinate action across roles and levels?
- What makes an organization robust, brittle, adaptive, or stagnant?
- Which management concepts travel well across public and private settings, and which do not?

## Canonical anchors

- general introductions to organization studies
- classic and contemporary management theory
- bureaucracy and organizational design literatures
- decision, coordination, and workflow studies
- organizational learning and adaptation work
- strategy, implementation, and performance discussions
