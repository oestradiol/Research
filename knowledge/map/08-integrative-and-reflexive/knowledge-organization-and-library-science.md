# Knowledge Organization and Library Science

## Cluster link

- [emerges-from](../../_relations/emerges-from.md) [08 Integrative and Reflexive](README.md)

## Study purpose

Study classification, retrieval, metadata, indexing, and the organization of knowledge systems that remain usable rather than merely tidy.

## Key questions

- How should large knowledge graphs be structured and retrieved?
- What makes a classification system usable rather than merely clean?
- How should metadata, links, and navigational surfaces work together?

## Canonical anchors

- introductions to knowledge organization
- library science and indexing foundations
- metadata design and retrieval literatures
- classification and taxonomy debates
- information architecture and findability materials
- digital knowledge system design work
