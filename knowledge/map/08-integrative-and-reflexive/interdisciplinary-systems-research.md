# Interdisciplinary Systems Research

## Cluster link

- [emerges-from](../../_relations/emerges-from.md) [08 Integrative and Reflexive](README.md)

## Study purpose

Study cross-domain systems work, integration problems, and disciplined framework comparison.

## Key questions

- How should multiple disciplines be linked without flattening them?
- What makes a systems framework genuinely useful across domains?
- How do we distinguish productive integration from vague synthesis talk?

## Canonical anchors

- interdisciplinary systems introductions
- cross-domain systems methodology materials
- integration and synthesis literatures
- complexity and systems bridge work
- comparative framework evaluation materials
- critiques of shallow interdisciplinarity
