## Study purpose

Study wholes, parts, organization, interaction, coupling, and system-level behavior across domains.

## Key questions

- When does a system-level view reveal something real?
- How should boundaries and coupling be drawn?
- What makes a systems vocabulary explanatory rather than merely expansive?

## Canonical anchors

- general systems theory overviews
- systems-thinking primers
- organization and management systems readings
- open systems and boundary-setting literatures
- cross-domain systems methodology
- critiques of vague or inflationary systems language
